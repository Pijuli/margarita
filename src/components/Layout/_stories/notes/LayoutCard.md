# Layout Cards

Margarita has two different cards that are used as <kbd>content containers</kbd>. They can be:

## White cards

They have:
Padding of 24px
White background
Shadow

## Gray cards

They have:
Padding of 24px
Gray background
No shadow


## Extra prop

This component has the possibility of adding a top margin of 24px by adding the prop `has-margin-top`
